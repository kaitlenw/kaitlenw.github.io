About Me

Hi! I'm Kaitlen, a fourth year undergraduate studying an integrated Masters degree (MEng) in Computer Science at the University of Warwick. My main interests are in web development and game development, but I'm always open to a challenge, and learning something new.

In my spare time, I train Pokémon; recently I became the Galar region Champion and even caught the fabled Zacian. In addition, I am a seasoned and competitive go-karter, and can often be found speeding down Rainbow Road. I'm also occasionally called upon to save the Kingdom of Hyrule from the grasps of the evil Ganon. In the past, I have travelled across land and even universe to save Princess Peach, and rescued my brother from a haunted hotel with only a glorified vacuum cleaner to defend myself. Any enquiries and/or requests should be directed to my secretary, Isabelle.

When I'm not saving people and places from unrest, you can find me listening to or even playing music. I recently saw Bastille in concert and the West End musical Waitress, and I can also play the clarinet!
On rainy days, you may find me curled up with hot chocolate and a good book. I'm particularly interested in the people who played a part in the history of modern science (for example, I've read Alan Turing: The Enigma by Andrew Hodges and Hidden Figures by Margot Lee Shetterly), however I also enjoy fictional adventures including classics such as Sherlock Holmes. I also enjoy more modern writing such as Angels and Demons by Dan Brown or The Priory of the Orange Tree by Samantha Shannon.
