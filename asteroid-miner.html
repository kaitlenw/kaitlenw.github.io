Asteroid Miner

A clone of Asteroid Miner, a game where the aim is to gather materials from asteroids to sell and upgrade your spaceship.

Back To All Projects

Gameplay of the orginal game can be viewed here.

In Asteroid Miner, you control a spaceship, to fly around the galaxy, destroying asteroids and collecting the materials found within them. Selling these materials at a nearby planet allows you to replenish fuel, make repairs to the ship and purchase other upgrades.

The game is written in C#, using the Unity game engine, and graphics and audio mostly from Kenney.nl. The game is currently in the early stages of development, and this page will be updated with more development details as it progresses.

Currently, the following features have been implemented for the game:
  • Ship movement and collisions with asteroids
  • Shooting, and laser collisions with asteroids
  • Asteroid destruction and material dropping
  • Material collection, and inventory displaying
  • Landing on planets and selling materials
